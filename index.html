Frequently Asked Questions

What do the scales mean?

Scales represent the ratio of the model to the real car. A 1:18 model is 18 times smaller than the actual car (approx. 25-30cm). A 1:24 is 24 times smaller (approx. 18-20cm), and 1:64 is 64 times smaller (approx. 6-8cm, pocket-sized).

Do the wheels steer and doors open?

In most high-end 1:18 models (like AutoArt), the steering wheel turns the wheels, and doors, hoods, and trunks open. In 1:64 models, the body is usually sealed to ensure perfect scaling structure, though they feature rolling rubber tires.

How are models shipped?

We wrap all collector boxes in heavy double-wall cardboard boxes with dense air-cushion packaging to guarantee the retail box arrives in mint condition.

Return & Exchange Policy

We offer a 30-day mint-in-box return policy. If your scale model is defective or has paint rash out of the box, we will ship a replacement model immediately and cover the return shipping costs.

Returns for change of mind must be unopened in original manufacturer packaging with security seals intact.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is vital to us. We do not sell or trade collector profiles. We encrypt all payment transactions, and details of your collection showcase are strictly confidential.
